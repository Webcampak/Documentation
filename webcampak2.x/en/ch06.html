To ease debugging, Webcampak record a large set of logs during its operation.

You will have access to various tabs:

  • Capture logs: logs captured during Webcampak picture acquisition process

  • Daily Video logs

  • Custom Video logs

  • Post-Prod. logs

  • Last configuration changes: all configuration changes made to the source since the beginning.

We strongly advise you to read logs whenever an issu appears or simply to ensure that everything is running smoothly.

1. Lecture des logs

Logs details all major actions performed by Webcampak during its operations.

11 May 2012 - 11:54:11 - gphoto(1-1267): Imagemagick: Crop: Zone size: 3904x2807 Position: x: 726 y: 7

A line of log is compose by::

  • 11 May 2012 - 11:54:11 : Date of the action

  • gphoto: Kind of source (gphoto, webcam, ipcam, webfile, rtsp, phidget)

  • (1-1267): source ID - System process #

  • Imagemagick: Action type or module

  • Crop: Zone size: 3904x2807 Position: x: 726 y: 7 : Details about the action

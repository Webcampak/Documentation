2. Permissions window

This window let you configure users, sources and all related permissions.

When you open permission window you will have access to four different tabs:

  • Users: to add/edit/delete users and manage access to sources.

  • Groups: to add/edit/delete groups, manage group members and group permissions.

  • Companies: to add/edit/delete companies. This configuration is only for administrative purposes and does not have any impact on Webcampak.

  • Sources: to add/edit/delete sources and manage members.

When you first configure a Webcampak, first start by creating a company and a group before starting to configure users as you will have to select a group and a company when a new user is being created.

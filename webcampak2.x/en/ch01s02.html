2. Webcampak & Webcampak Cloud

Webcampak is an automated device to capture, process and store pictures.

Initially created to control DSLR cameras, our solutions are now composed by two products:

  • Webcampak: an all-weather automated appliance aimed to be installed outdoor and compatible with a large set of capture device (DSLR cameras, IP cameras, other Webcampak, ...)

  • Webcampak Cloud: a datacenter-hoster automated appliance aimed to capture, process and store pictures and videos captured from IP Cameras or other Webcampaks.

2.1. Webcampak

Please do not hesitate to visit our website (http://www.webcampak.com) for more details about our products.

2.1.1. Capture devices

Webcampak is able to capture pictures from the following devices:

  • DSLR and point-and-shoot cameras

  • USB webcams

  • Web pictures

  • IP cameras / Webcampak

  • Streaming sources

  • Pictures captured during an offline timelapse (setup with camera + intervalometer)

Being compatible with high-end DSLR cameras, Webcampak can process very high quality pictures when connected to an appropriate camera (not all DSLR are compatible).

Canon point-and-shoot cameras, sold after 2009, are not compatible anymore but you can still find such camera on the second-hand market for a fraction of their original price.

2.1.2. Communication

Webcampak has been conceived right from the beginning to be network-enabled. It is compatible with various types of links:

  • GPRS/3G/4G

  • Wi-Fi (2,4 or 5 Ghz)

  • Ethernet

  • Satellite

Some Internet links (especially wireless) are subject to FUP (Fair Use Policy) limiting the amount of data to be transmitted each month. Webcampak can fit almost any FUP limitations by:

  • Sending a fraction of captured pictures (for example, send 1 out of 5 pictures)

  • Reducing definition of pictures sent over network

  • Cropping pictures

By adjusting those parameters you can limit the amount of data to be transferred and therefore be compliant with your provider's FUP.

2.1.3. Picture quality

Webcampak is compatible with all Canon DSLR cameras and associated lenses.

Picture quality during day or night time will be very close (if not identical) to quality that can be obtained by a professional photographer for a single shot.

Take in consideration that some cameras and/or lenses might required Webcampak adjustments (for example a larger enclosure due to lens size).

4. Downloading a video

To download a video, simply right-click on the green arrow in the list and select "Save link as" or any similar option depending of your web browser.

Please note that only the H.264 (highest quality) video will be offered for download. If you want to download the MP4 video, please connect to your source using a FTP client.

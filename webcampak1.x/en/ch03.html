Chapter 3. First steps

Webcampak is provide with two separate interfaces:

  • viewer panel: to browse within pictures and videos

  • administration panel: to configure Webcampak and its sources.

A user management system will let you choose which user can access which part of Webcampak.

To access Webcampak for the first time, open your web browser and connect to: http://Webcampak-IP-address, replacing "Webcampak-IP-address" by the actual Webcampak IP.

Username/Password: root/webcampak

Important

Please change the password as soon as possible.
